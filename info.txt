== Description ==
The Followers Social Bar section allows you to show the followers/subscribers from your social networks.

You can use it in the content area or in the morefoot area, has 4 themes and 4 icons sets for choose from.

Besides has a internal cache system to maximize the response time, in this way the requests to the APIs are made only 1 time per hour.

=== Social Networks and services === 

* Facebook
* Twitter
* FeedBurner
* Youtube
* Vimeo
* Digg
